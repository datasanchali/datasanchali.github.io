About Me

I am a senior analytics engineer and data scientist with over 7 years of experience helping Fortune 500 companies drive strategic growth through data-driven insights.

I've held roles across data science and data engineering at organizations like Republic Services, HSAG Inc. and HSBC Global Technology.

I hold an M.S. in Business Analytics from Arizona State University and B. Tech in ECE from Galgotias University.

Having lived and worked in both the U.S. and India, I bring a global perspective to my work and an understanding of diverse markets.

Besides data science and storytelling, I like photography and traveling.

What I Do

As a seasoned analytics professional, I specialize in:

  • Designing robust and scalable data pipelines with tools like SQL, Python, and R
  • Building compelling data visualizations and interactive dashboards using Tableau and Power BI
  • Applying advanced statistical models and predictive analytics to uncover trends and predict outcomes.
